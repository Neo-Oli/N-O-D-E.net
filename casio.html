MODIFICATIONS

So this mod is based on the famous Casio F-91W digital watch, which is one of the most popular wristwatches in the world.

The standard face has been removed, and, replaced with a simple black design.

I went with the letterbox design instead of a rectangle, firstly because I like the aesthetics of showing some of the watch innards, but also because it exposes the LED.

And speaking of this LED, I swapped out the standard green one, for a brighter white LED. It's inkeeping with the monochrome design, and it's also bright enough to use as a little torch to find your keys or something in the dark.

I've also added an NFC chip into the body of the watch. It's an NTAG213 chip, with 137 Bytes of usable storage.

I actually didn't think this would work due to interference from the metal parts, but it does, you just have to scan the sweet spot, just above the screen.

Finally, I've added a micro SD card socket to the backplate of the watch, making it easy carry important data with you.

It sticks out a little bit, but it's not really noticeable when it's sitting on your wrist.
